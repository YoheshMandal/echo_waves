About the Band

Echo Waves is a contemporary rock band formed in 2020. Our music blends modern rock sounds with classical influences to create something truly unique.

With heartfelt lyrics and powerful melodies, we tell stories of life, love, and the human experience.

Our members bring diverse musical backgrounds together to create a sound that resonates with audiences everywhere.
